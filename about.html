OFFICIALLY FOUNDED IN 2019

By Asia Jackson, Anjelic Hair Care is an all-natural hair and skincare brand that strives to instill confidence within the natural hair community.

Ms. Jackson originally aspired to become an orthodontist until an accelerator program visited her high school during her junior year. She participated in the program, which equipped her with the tools to launch a successful business. After identifying a problem and seeking a solution, she collaborated with a chemist to formulate products that promote healthy hair growth.

Her first creation was the Organic Flaxseed Gelee, a lightweight formula that nourishes the hair follicles, prevents breakage, and soothes the scalp, supporting styles such as wash-and-go’s, twist-outs, puffs, braid-outs, locs, and updos. At the end of the program, she presented her product and won $1,000 to officially launch Anjelic Hair Care.

By her second year of business, she had five products in production and decided to focus on one product, the Multipurpose Shea Butter, while balancing her college workload. She continues to grow her business by investing in new technologies, promoting on social media, and selling at multiple vendor events.

Thank you for being a part of another page in our story.
